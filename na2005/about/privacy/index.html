Privacy Policy

Your email address will not be:

  • displayed on the website
  • used to send unsolicited (spam) email
  • sold, or passed on to other organisations

Your email address is required for the purposes of validating as we believe in responsibility and to prevent fraudulent use of the website.

Your email address is available to the site administrators.

Data entered in the text boxes on the site — except for email addresses — is visible to viewers of the website once the post has been confirmed.

Additional data is collected for the purposes of anonymised statistics which may or may not be made available to:

  • visitors of the website
  • developers
  • targeted interest groups
  • funding organisations
  • The Electoral Commission

The site operates as a project of mySociety, part of registered charity UKCOD.
